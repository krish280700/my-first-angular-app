Connecting Your Google Inbox

Overview

If you are using a single user email address (meaning that your email address is accessible through a single username and password), follow these steps to create a direct Gmail inbox integration with your Supermoon team inbox.

Steps

  1. Navigate to your AIFS Settings (bottom left of your screen)
  2. Click 'Linked Accounts'
  3. Click 'Connect with google'
  4. Navigate to your AIFS Settings (bottom left of your screen)
  5. Click 'Linked Accounts'
  6. Click 'Connect with google'

Once your Gmail inbox is connected, you will begin to see your messages import. If you have a lot of messages to import, it may take several hours to complete. Additionally, any new inbound emails to the connected Gmail account will start to appear in your AIFS inbox.
